Frequently Asked Questions

How do I buy tickets?

Simply browse events, select your preferred ticket type, and pay using Mobile Money (MTN or Airtel).

Is Mobile Money the only payment method?

Yes, Quenex exclusively uses Mobile Money (MTN & Airtel) for all transactions.

How do I become an event organizer?

Register as an organizer, then you can create and manage events on our platform.

What if I don't receive my ticket?

Contact our support team immediately via phone or WhatsApp for assistance.

Can I get a refund for my ticket?

Refund policies vary by event. Please check the event details or contact the event organizer directly.

How do I check in at an event?

Present your QR code ticket (digital or printed) at the venue entrance for scanning.
