About Quenex

Revolutionizing Event Management in Uganda

Our Mission

To provide a seamless, accessible, and efficient digital platform that connects event organizers with attendees in Uganda, eliminating manual inefficiencies and promoting economic growth through technology.

Our Vision

To become Uganda's leading events management platform, empowering secondary cities with digital solutions that transform how communities plan, promote, and participate in events.

The Challenge We're Solving

Manual Processes

Phone calls and physical bookings leading to overbooking and confusion

Limited Visibility

Events promoted only through posters and radio, missing potential attendees

Poor Record Keeping

Manual sales tracking leading to revenue loss and poor decision making

Our Solution

Web-Based Platform

Accessible from any device with internet connection

Mobile Money Integration

Seamless payments using MTN or Airtel Mobile Money

Real-Time Analytics

Track sales, attendance, and revenue instantly
